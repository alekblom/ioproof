IOProof

Cryptographic proof that an AI said what it said.

Merkle-batched on-chain attestation for AI interactions

IOProof is a proxy that sits between you and your AI provider. It hashes every request and response, batches proofs into a Merkle tree, and commits the root to Solana. One transaction proves thousands of interactions.

How it works

1 Send your request

Call our proxy instead of the AI API directly. We support OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, and Gemini.

2 We hash everything

SHA-256 hash of the exact request bytes and the exact response bytes. Deterministic and verifiable.

3 Merkle batching

Proofs are collected and batched into a Merkle tree. One Solana transaction commits the root, covering all proofs in the batch.

4 Verify any proof

Each proof includes a Merkle path back to the on-chain root. Verify independently without trusting us.

Supported providers

OpenAI Anthropic xAI Google Gemini

Quick start

curl -X POST https://ioproof.com/v1/proxy/openai/v1/chat/completions \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "X-Provider-Key: sk-your-openai-key" \
  -d '{
    "model": "gpt-4o",
    "messages": [{"role": "user", "content": "Hello"}]
  }'

Verify a proof

Paste any hash (request, response, or combined) to look up its on-chain attestation.
